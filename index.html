What are

TechnoTools?

TechnoTools are collection of web tools. A place where you can edit, manage, save or share your data more efficiently and easily.

Paper Mail

Paper Mail provides use & throw emails. It is a kind of email service that allows to receive email at a temporary address.

Easy Share

Easy Share is a free service that allows you to connect and share your images, videos and even texts between multiple devices.

PvtNote

PvtNote allows you to send secret messages which are encrypted and distruct itself once after viewing.

Redirect

Redirect is link shortening service that shortens your unwieldly links into more manageable and useable URLs with analytics.

IP Tracker

IP Tracker allows you to find, track, trace IP Address location and provides analytics

Fun-with-code

CodeEmu is online HTML playground which allows to write, edit and save your coding with LIVE preview!

Fun-with-Us

ApolloXY allows you to share your creative ideas and thoughts through illustrative images.
